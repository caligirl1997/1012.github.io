Artificial Intelligence

DESCRIPTION

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to any human-like intelligence demonstrated by computing technology.Capabilities of an AI machine include learning from examples and experience, object recognition, understanding and responding to language, etc. Common examples of AI would be Google maps, self-driving vehicles.

RELEVANCE

AI has been implemented in every facet in our daily lives, and as a result society has seen a dramatic increase in productivity. AI have taken over repetitive or dangerous tasks, freeing up the human workforce to focus on tasks that require creativity or empathy. We utilize AI without realizing - Google Maps, smart searching, etc. A.I. is still a relatively young concept, which is constantly evolving and improving by the day.

IMPORTANCE

Over the past years the intellect of AI has increased frequently, having the ability to help advance the human knowledge of this world by solving problems that may be too complex for humans. However, because humans give such technologies the ability of human-like minded/behaviours, this therefore allows AI to possess a moral standard.
