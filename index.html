Artificial Intelligence

DESCRIPTION

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to any human-like intelligence demonstrated by computing technology.

RELEVANCE

AI has been implemented in every facet in our daily lives, and as a result society has seen a dramatic increase in productivity. AI have taken over repetitive or dangerous tasks, freeing up the human workforce to focus on tasks that require creativity or empathy. We utilize AI without realizing - Google Maps, smart searching, etc.
